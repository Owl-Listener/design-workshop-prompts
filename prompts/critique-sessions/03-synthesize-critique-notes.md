# Synthesize Critique Notes into Action Items

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
Here are the raw notes from our design critique:

[Paste all feedback]

Synthesize this into:
1. Feedback themes (group similar comments together)
2. Conflicts (where reviewers disagree, and what's driving the disagreement)
3. A prioritized action list: what to change now, what to explore, what to park
4. Any feedback that's actually a requirements question (and who to ask)
5. One thing multiple people praised that I should protect as I iterate
```
