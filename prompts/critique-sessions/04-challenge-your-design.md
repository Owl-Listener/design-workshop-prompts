# Challenge Your Own Design Before Critique

**Role:** Participant

## Prompt

```
I'm about to put this design into critique: [describe your design and its intent]

Play the role of 4 different critics, each with a distinct lens:
1. The user advocate (is this actually solving a real problem?)
2. The systems thinker (how does this affect the broader product?)
3. The accessibility reviewer (who might this exclude?)
4. The skeptic (what's the weakest assumption here?)

For each perspective, give me the single toughest question they'd ask. Then tell me which one I should have the best answer for before I walk into the room.
```
