# Turn Vague Feedback into Specific Direction

**Role:** Both

## Prompt

```
I received this feedback on my design: "[paste the vague or confusing feedback]"

Help me decode it:
1. What they're probably actually saying (translate from vague to specific)
2. What design element or decision is most likely triggering this reaction
3. Two concrete things I could try in response
4. A clarifying question I could ask them to confirm I'm reading it right
```
