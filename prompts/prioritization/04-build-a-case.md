# Build a Case for Your Priority

**Role:** Participant

## Prompt

```
I need to argue that [feature/initiative] should be prioritized higher. My audience is [who] and they care about [their priorities].

Help me build a 2-minute pitch that:
- Opens with the user problem (make them feel it)
- Connects to a metric or outcome they already care about
- Addresses the most likely objection ("but what about [competing priority]?")
- Proposes a scope that's small enough to say yes to
- Ends with a specific ask, not a vague "we should think about this"

Keep it honest. If the case is weak, tell me where to strengthen it.
```
