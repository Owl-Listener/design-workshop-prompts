# Sprint Retrospective Synthesis

**Role:** Both

## Prompt

```
We just finished a design sprint. Here's what happened:

[Paste: what we built, what we tested, key findings, team reactions]

Help me write a sprint retrospective that covers:
1. What we learned (the actual insights, not just "users liked it")
2. What surprised us
3. What we'd do differently next sprint
4. The clearest next step coming out of this
5. A one-paragraph "elevator pitch" summary I can send to leadership
```
