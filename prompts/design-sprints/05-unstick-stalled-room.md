# Unstick a Stalled Sprint Room

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
My sprint team is stuck. We're on [day/phase] and the energy has dropped because [describe what happened: can't agree, too many ideas, solution feels obvious/boring, scope creep, etc.].

The room has [number] people: [roles].

Give me:
1. A diagnosis of what's likely going wrong (based on common sprint dynamics)
2. A 10-minute exercise I can run right now to shift the energy
3. A reframe of the problem that might unlock new thinking
4. The one question I should ask the room to get things moving again
```
