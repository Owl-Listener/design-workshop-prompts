# Translate Design Roadmap for Leadership

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
I need to present our design roadmap to [VP/Director/C-level]. They care about [business outcomes, timelines, risk, team health — pick what matters most].

Here's the roadmap:
[Paste it]

Translate it into a leadership-ready format:
1. A 3-sentence executive summary (outcome-focused, not activity-focused)
2. Each initiative reframed as "this enables [business outcome]" not "we're designing [feature]"
3. The top 2 risks and what we're doing about them
4. What we're intentionally NOT doing and why (this builds trust)
5. One decision or resource we need from them to stay on track
```
