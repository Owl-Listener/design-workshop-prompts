# Run a Quarterly Planning Workshop

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
I'm facilitating a quarterly design planning workshop. The team is [size and roles]. We have [time].

Input: [what's carrying over, new requests, research insights, leadership priorities]

Design a workshop agenda that:
1. Opens with a 10-minute reflection on last quarter (what we shipped, what we learned, what we didn't get to)
2. Includes an exercise to surface new opportunities the team sees
3. Has a structured way to evaluate and compare opportunities
4. Ends with clear commitments: what we'll do, what we won't, and who owns what
5. Builds in time for the "uncomfortable conversation" (the thing nobody wants to say about capacity, scope, or strategy)

Include facilitation notes: how to keep it moving, what to do if it stalls, and how to handle the loudest voice in the room.
```
