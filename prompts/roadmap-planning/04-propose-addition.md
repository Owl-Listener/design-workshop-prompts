# Propose Adding Something to the Roadmap

**Role:** Participant

## Prompt

```
I want to propose adding [initiative/project] to our design roadmap. Here's my case:

- The problem: [what's broken or missing]
- Who it affects: [users, internal teams, business metrics]
- Evidence: [research, data, complaints, competitive pressure]
- Rough effort: [my best guess]

Help me write a one-page proposal that:
1. Frames this in terms of what the team already cares about (don't introduce new goals)
2. Shows what it would replace or what else would need to shift
3. Proposes the smallest useful version (don't ask for the moon)
4. Anticipates the "why now?" question with a clear answer
5. Includes a "what if we don't" scenario that makes the cost of inaction concrete
```
