# Stress-Test Your Roadmap

**Role:** Both

## Prompt

```
Here's our current design roadmap:

[Paste your roadmap]

Stress-test it by asking:
1. If our top priority takes 2x longer than expected, what breaks?
2. What happens if we lose a team member for 6 weeks?
3. Which items are we doing because of inertia vs. genuine strategic value?
4. Is there a dependency we haven't named that could block everything?
5. What would a new design director cut from this on their first day?
6. Are we solving last quarter's problem or this quarter's?

Give me the honest version, not the comfortable one.
```
