# Write a Roadmap Narrative

**Role:** Both

## Prompt

```
Here's our roadmap as a list of items:

[Paste the list]

Turn it into a narrative. Not a bulleted list with timeframes, but a story:
- Why are we doing these things in this order?
- What user experience are we building toward?
- What's the "before" and "after" for our users 6 months from now?
- How does each phase build on the last?

Write it as 3-4 paragraphs I could put at the top of a planning doc or read at the start of a team meeting. It should make someone who just joined the team understand not just what we're doing, but why it matters.
```
