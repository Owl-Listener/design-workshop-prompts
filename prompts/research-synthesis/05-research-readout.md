# Write a Research Readout That Moves People

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
I need to present research findings to [audience: leadership / product team / engineering / cross-functional]. They have [15/30/45] minutes and care most about [what they want to know].

My key findings are:
[Paste your findings]

Write me a presentation outline that:
- Opens with the single most compelling finding (not methodology)
- Uses participant quotes strategically (max 3-4)
- Builds to a clear "so what" moment
- Ends with specific, actionable recommendations (not just "more research needed")
- Includes talking points for each section

The goal is for people to leave the room ready to act, not just informed.
```
