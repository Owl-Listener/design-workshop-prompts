# Create a Persona from Research Data

**Role:** Both

## Prompt

```
Based on this research data:

[Paste interview excerpts, survey data, behavioral observations]

Create a research-backed persona that includes:
- A name and realistic context (not a stereotype)
- Their primary goal and what's blocking them
- Behaviors I actually observed (not assumed)
- A direct quote that captures their mindset
- The key tension in their experience (what they want vs. what they have to do)
- How they differ from the other user types we've identified: [list them]

Make this persona feel like a real person, not a marketing archetype.
```
