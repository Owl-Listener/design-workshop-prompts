# Find What You're Missing in Your Research

**Role:** Participant

## Prompt

```
I've been synthesizing research on [topic]. Here's what I've found so far:

[Paste your current themes/insights]

My research included: [methods used, number of participants, who they were]

Help me identify:
1. Perspectives I might be missing (whose voice isn't represented?)
2. Questions my current insights don't answer
3. Potential biases in my sample or methods
4. Where I'm making a leap from data to conclusion that needs more evidence
5. One provocative "what if the opposite were true?" challenge to my strongest finding
```
