# Build a Journey Map from Research Data

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
Based on this research about [user type] doing [task/process]:

[Paste your research data: quotes, observations, survey responses]

Build a journey map that includes:
- 5-7 phases of their experience
- What they're doing, thinking, and feeling at each phase
- Pain points and bright spots
- The emotional arc (where does frustration peak? where does confidence grow?)
- Opportunities for design intervention at each phase

Format it as a table I can paste into a collaborative doc or whiteboard.
```
