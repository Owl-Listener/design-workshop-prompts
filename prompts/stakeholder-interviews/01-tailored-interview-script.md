# Generate a Tailored Interview Script

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
I'm interviewing [role/name] about [project or initiative]. They care most about [known priorities]. I have 30 minutes.

Generate an interview script with:
- 2 warm-up questions to build rapport
- 5 core questions that surface their real concerns (not just stated priorities)
- 2 questions that test assumptions we've been making
- 1 closing question that opens the door to things I haven't thought to ask

For each question, include a brief note on what I'm actually listening for in their answer.
```

## Tips

Replace the bracketed sections with your specifics. The more context you give, the sharper the questions.
