# Compare Perspectives Across Multiple Interviews

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
I've interviewed these stakeholders about [project]:

[Paste summaries or key quotes from each person]

Compare their perspectives and show me:
1. Where they genuinely agree (shared ground I can build on)
2. Where they think they agree but actually mean different things
3. Where they openly disagree and what's driving the disagreement
4. The single biggest risk if I design without resolving these tensions
```
