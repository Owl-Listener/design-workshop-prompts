# Prepare Your Own Talking Points

**Role:** Participant

## Prompt

```
I'm a [your role] about to be interviewed by the design team about [project]. I want to communicate clearly and make sure my perspective is actually useful.

Help me prepare:
- 3 things I should definitely mention (based on what design teams typically need to hear from someone in my role)
- 2 things I should be honest about even if they're uncomfortable
- A way to frame my constraints without sounding like I'm blocking progress
```
