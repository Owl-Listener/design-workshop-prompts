# Turn Interview Insights into Design Principles

**Role:** Both

## Prompt

```
Based on these stakeholder interview themes:

[Paste your themes or summary]

Draft 4-6 design principles that:
- Are specific enough to actually guide decisions (not generic "be user-friendly")
- Each one resolves a real tension that came up in the interviews
- Include a "this, not that" example for each one
- Could be printed on a wall and used during design reviews
```
