# Debrief and Extract Themes from a Session

**Role:** Facilitator

## Prompt

```
Here are my raw notes from a stakeholder interview:

[Paste your notes]

Analyze this and give me:
1. The 3 strongest themes across their answers
2. Any contradictions between what they said they want and what they actually seem to need
3. Assumptions they're making that we should test
4. A one-paragraph summary I could share with my team
5. Two follow-up questions I should ask next time
```
